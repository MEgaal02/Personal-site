Egaal Software Solutions was founded in 2022 with a clear mission: build software that works, scales, and drives real business value.

Our Story

Egaal Software Solutions started as a vision to provide businesses with high-quality, affordable software development. Founded by Mahamood Egaal — a Full-Stack Software Engineer with deep experience in Python, Django, and modern web technologies — the company was built on the belief that every business deserves well-crafted software.

Operating as a trading name of TaxGen AI Ltd, we bring the credibility and structure of a registered limited company while maintaining the agility of a focused software consultancy. From our early days building e-learning platforms and AI-powered applications, we've grown into a team that handles end-to-end web development for clients across industries.

Today, we continue to expand our capabilities while staying true to our founding principles: clean architecture, transparent communication, and delivering on every commitment.

Company Structure

TaxGen AI Ltd

Egaal Software Solutions operates as a trading name of TaxGen AI Ltd, a registered limited company. TaxGen AI is also our flagship product — a collaborative, chat room-style platform providing AI-assisted guidance for tax affairs.
